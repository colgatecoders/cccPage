PROGRAMMING WORKSHOPS

Each workshop is hosted by a fellow Colgate student and is a hands-on programming session designed to introduce you to a new programming language or framework. There are three levels of proficiency:

  1. BEGINNER: no coding experience required (open to ALL majors)

  2. INTERMEDIATE: some coding experience required (CS 101/102)

  3. ADVANCED: working knowledge of programming languages and frameworks OR attendance to our Beginner and Intermediate level workshops
